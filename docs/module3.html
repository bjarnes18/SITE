Module 3: Business Student of Today

Summary of Marketing Management

Part 1: Fundamentals of Marketing

  • What is Marketing? - Marketing is defined as the process of identifying customer needs and satisfying them better than the competition. Example: Easy access to information and affordable furniture.
  • Customer-Centricity: Customers must be at the center of marketing strategies to meet their needs.

Understanding the Market

Market Forces Changing Marketing:

  • Technology: E-commerce, social media, AI, and data-driven marketing.
  • Globalization: Multicultural markets, increased competition, and changing customer needs.
  • Environment: Impact of climate change and pandemics.
  • Social Responsibility: Ethics, environmental impact, and legal considerations.

The 4 P's of Marketing

  • Product: What is offered to the customer.
  • Price: What the customer pays for the product.
  • Promotion: How the product is marketed.
  • Place: Where the product is available.
